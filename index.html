Who I Am

Background & Skills

I'm Sajeela Luqman, a mathematics educator and MBAI graduate student at Ontario Tech University based in Toronto. My academic background spans pure and applied mathematics, and I'm currently building expertise at the intersection of AI, data visualization, and responsible technology.

My work explores how data can be turned into compelling stories — from redesigning 19th-century epidemiology maps to investigating how patients and clinicians build trust in AI-powered medical imaging.

I bring classroom experience from Kindergarten to university level, analytical rigor from years of mathematical research, and a drive to communicate findings in ways that genuinely matter.
